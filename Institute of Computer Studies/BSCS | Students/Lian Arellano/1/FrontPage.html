Welcome to AppDate!

Hi! This is AppDate, a learning management website with an integrated system. This is used by the students of RTU-SHS and allowed them to collaborate, time manage, and remind individuals in online learning process.

Frequently Asked Questions

An active virtual classroom and an engaging online learning environment for instructors and students are the goals of AppDate, a learning management platform with an integrated system that has been thoughtfully planned and created to provide an effective learning structure in remote learning.

Click "search courses," type in the course name to find the exact course you're looking for, click the course and enter the code your professor gave you.

By keeping track of significant dates, assisting with planning, and connecting with other platform features, the calendar tool helps students and teachers stay organized, communicate, and manage their time effectively.

Reminders of tasks and forthcoming events will be sent to you through Gmail.

With AppDate's administrative feature, you can forget your password and have an action along with updates sent to your institutional email.
